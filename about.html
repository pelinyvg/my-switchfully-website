About me

Let me introduce myself a little more on this page. My fullname is Pelin Yüzbaşıoğlu Van Gossum. I am 30 years old. I come from Izmir, Turkey (a coastal city in the western part of Turkey). I got my bachelor degree in tourism management in Turkey. After graduation I moved to Belgium. I've been living in Leuven since 2013. During my first year in Belgium, I learned Dutch and then studied master of tourism at the KU Leuven.


Before deciding to become a software developer, I was working in administrative jobs for about three years. Programming started out as a hobby at first, but I loved it more than I did my current job at the time, so I decided to switch my carreer around.


Aside from my passion in software developing, I like gaming, baking, running, travelling and hiking.
